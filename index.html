NATURE

Nature, in the broadest sense, is the natural world, physical universe, material world or material universe. "Nature" refers to the phenomena of the physical world, and also to life in general.

EARTH

Earth is the fifth largest planet in the solar system and the only known place in the universe to support life.

LIFE

Life is characterized by organization, metabolism, growth, adaptation, response to stimuli and reproduction.
